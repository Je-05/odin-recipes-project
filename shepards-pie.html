Shepard's Pie:

Shepherd's Pie is a classic, hearty dish originating from the UK, traditionally made with minced or ground lamb. It’s distinct for its layered structure, with the base made of a rich meat filling topped with creamy mashed potatoes. Here's a detailed breakdown:

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef(Cottage Pie) or Lamb(Shepards Pie)
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Brown Gravy

Cooking Steps:

  1. Preheat Your Oven to 350 Degrees.
  2. Dice the Carrots & Onions into small Cubes.
  3. Wash, Peel, and Cube the Potatoes.
  4. In a Medium-Sized Pot, Add the Potatoes & Bring 4 cups of water to a Boil.
  5. In a Medium-Sized Pan, Brown the Ground Lamb (Ground Beef If Making Cottage Pie) Until a Golden Brown Fond Forms.
  6. Remove the Lamb (Or Beef) and 1 TBSP of Butter to the Pan.
  7. Add the Onions & Carrots and Sauté till soft
  8. Check on the Potatoes, If They are Done (A Simple Fork Through The Middle Will Do the Trick)' Drain the Water Using A Colander.
  9. Add The Potatoes back Into the Pot, Then Add 4 TBSP of Butter and Enough Cream To Make It Creamy and Fluffy.
  10. Add the Brown Gravy, Ground Lamb (Or Beef) Into the Pan With the Onions & Carrots And Bring To A Simmer
  11. Using A Little Bit of Butter, Grease Your Dutch Oven.
  12. Then Add Your Lamb (Beef) to the Dutch Oven.
  13. TakeYour Mashed Potatoes And Layer It Across Your Filling.
  14. Finally Put It Into the Oven for 15-20 Minutes And Enjoy!
